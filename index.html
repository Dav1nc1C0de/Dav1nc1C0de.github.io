Wedding Day Schedule

Roles and Responsibilities

Point Person

The main point of contact for vendors and the keeper of the master schedule. They will help oversee everything and troubleshoot any issues.

Guest Greeter & Guide

This friendly face will welcome guests, answer their questions, and make sure they feel comfortable and know where to go.

Tech & Ambiance Lead

This person is in charge of the sound system, music cues, and overall vibe of the space.

Activities Coordinator

This friend will manage the fun interactive elements like the instant camera and guest book.
